Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your username and hashed password
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Basic usage data: pages visited, to help us improve the app

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly and provide you with the features you use:

  • To display your expenses, summaries, and spending breakdowns
  • To authenticate you when you sign in
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the app. No personal or financial data is shared with any third party.

We do not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app. We take privacy concerns seriously and will respond promptly.
